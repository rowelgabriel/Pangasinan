💡 Cape Bolinao Lighthouse

Standing tall on Patar’s rocky cliff, the Cape Bolinao Lighthouse is one of the most iconic landmarks in Pangasinan. Built during the American period, it offers a panoramic view of the West Philippine Sea. The lighthouse’s old-world charm and scenic surroundings make it a favorite spot for photography and sightseeing. It beautifully captures the maritime heritage and natural beauty of Bolinao.
