🏝 Hundred Islands

Located in Alaminos City, the Hundred Islands National Park is a stunning group of 124 islands scattered across the Lingayen Gulf. Each island offers unique experiences — from white-sand beaches and snorkeling spots to cliff-jumping adventures. Popular islands like Governor’s Island and Quezon Island are perfect for both relaxation and exploration. It’s a true paradise for nature lovers and thrill-seekers alike.

📍 Where to Find Hundred Islands

Located in Alaminos City, Pangasinan, just a few hours north of Manila.
