About Pangasinan
Pangasinan, located in the heart of Northern Luzon, is a province blessed with breathtaking natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Known for its pristine beaches, the world-famous Hundred Islands, and warm, welcoming people, it offers both adventure and tranquility. Visitors can explore its scenic landscapes, taste its flavorful local delicacies, and experience traditions deeply rooted in history. Whether you seek relaxation or exploration, Pangasinan is a destination that captures the essence of true Filipino charm.
