🪨 Enchanted Cave

The Enchanted Cave in Bolinao lives up to its magical name, featuring crystal-clear waters inside a coral limestone formation. Once part of an ancient seabed, the cave now attracts visitors with its cool natural pool perfect for swimming. Stalactites and unique rock formations add to its mystical appeal. It’s a refreshing and otherworldly escape deep within nature’s heart.

📍 Where to Find Enchanted Cave

The Enchanted Cave is a stunning natural attraction in Bolinao, Pangasinan — famous for its crystal-clear underground pool and captivating limestone formations.
